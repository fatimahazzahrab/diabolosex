Places that are Broken

Cities such as Tiberias (18 April 1948), Haifa (22 April), Safed (11 May), Jaffa (13 May), Acre (18 May), and Beersheba (21 October), and hundreds of towns and villages, were destroyed or depopulated. Thousands of Palestinians were killed in dozens of massacres, including at Balad al-Shaykh (December 1947), Deir Yassin (April 1948), Tantura (May), Lydda and Ramle (July), Safsaf (October), and Dawayima (October).

These People Loses Everything; their families, their cities, their land.
